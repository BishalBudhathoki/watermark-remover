How to Download TikTok Content

Single Video Download:

  1. Open the TikTok video you want to download
  2. Click the "Share" button and copy the video link
  3. Paste the link in the video URL field above
  4. Click "Download Video"

Profile Download:

  1. Visit the TikTok profile you want to download from
  2. Copy the profile URL from your browser's address bar
  3. Paste the URL in the profile URL field above
  4. Click "Download Profile Content"
