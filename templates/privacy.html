Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Social Media Manager and social media management service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Social media integration data
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Images and videos you upload for processing
  • Technical information (IP address, browser type, device information)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process your media files
  • To integrate with social media platforms
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your uploaded media files are temporarily stored for processing and automatically deleted after completion.

4. Social Media Integration

When you connect social media accounts, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. We adhere to each platform's terms of service and data usage policies.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Revoke social media access

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for:

  • Social media integration (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
  • Analytics and monitoring
  • Payment processing (if applicable)

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze service usage.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or service notification.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support channels.
